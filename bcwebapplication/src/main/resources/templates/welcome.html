BookCrossing (also BC, BCing or BXing) is defined as "the practice of leaving a book in a public place to be picked up and read by others, who then do likewise."

Although, we don't leave your book in a public place, it is delivered to a special box, which saves the value of the book and makes it more comfortable for You.

We also believe it is a huge step towards conscious consumption.
